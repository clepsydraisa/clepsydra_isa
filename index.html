Welcome to Path4Med

This site tracks the progress of the Path4Med project (Horizon-IA 101156867), conducted at the Instituto Superior de Agronomia (ISA) and Quinta da Cholda, Golegã, from February to July 2025. The project focuses on innovative solutions to address water and soil pollution in the Mediterranean Agro-Hydro-System, under the supervision of Prof. Maria do Rosário Cameira and Prof. Manuel Campagnolo.
