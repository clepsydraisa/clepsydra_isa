Welcome to Path4Med

This site tracks the progress of the Path4Med project (Horizon-IA 101156867), conducted at the Instituto Superior de Agronomia (ISA) and Quinta da Cholda, Golegã, from February to July 2025. The project focuses on innovative solutions to address water and soil pollution in the Mediterranean Agro-Hydro-System, under the supervision of Prof. Maria do Rosário Cameira, Prof. João Rolim and Prof. Manuel Campagnolo.

Explore the sections below to view the project timeline, resources, and reports.

Demonstration Site

Path4Med Demonstration Sites are crucial for tackling soil and water pollution in Mediterranean agricultural landscapes by testing innovative practices, monitoring technologies, and policies. These ‘living laboratories’ enable researchers, farmers, and stakeholders to evaluate sustainable solutions in action.

One key site is the Tejo Vulnerable Zone in Portugal, including Quinta da Cholda, Golegã. Led by the Instituto Superior de Agronomia (ISA), this site focuses on monitoring ground and surface water and developing effective agronomical practices to mitigate pollution in the Mediterranean Agro-Hydro-System.

D.G.5.: Develop a monitoring system regarding the ground and surface water status in different scenarios and identify, adapt, and evaluate effective agronomical practices so that the mitigation strategies can be efficiently designed and applied.
