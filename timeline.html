Project Overview

This page tracks the progress of tasks for the Path4Med project, conducted at the Instituto Superior de Agronomia (ISA) and Quinta da Cholda, Golegã, from February to July 2025. The work is supervised by Prof. Maria do Rosário Cameira and Prof. Manuel Campagnolo, focusing on innovative solutions to water and soil pollution in the Mediterranean Agro-Hydro-System.
